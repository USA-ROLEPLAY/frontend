About Our Community

Who we are and what we stand for

Community First

We're a passionate group of gamers who believe in creating free, high-quality gaming experiences for everyone. Our servers are community-funded and maintained by volunteers.

Free to Play

All our servers are completely free to access. We don't believe in pay-to-win mechanics. Our goal is to provide fair, enjoyable gameplay for all skill levels.

Growing Together

Starting with Minecraft, we're expanding to DayZ, Rust, and more. Our community helps decide which games we support next through polls and discussions.
