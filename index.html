Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our community

Our Minecraft server is whitelisted to maintain a quality community. To join:

1. Join our Discord server
2. Read the rules in #server-rules
3. Fill out the application in #whitelist-application
4. Wait for staff approval (usually within 24 hours)
5. Connect to play.usa-roleplay.org

We welcome players of all ages, but we do require maturity regardless of age. Our community is 13+ as per Discord's Terms of Service. Players under 13 cannot join our Discord but may still apply for the Minecraft server with parent/guardian permission.

Absolutely not! We firmly believe in fair play. All gameplay advantages must be earned through skill and time investment. We do offer cosmetic donations (like special particle effects or name colors) to help support server costs, but these provide no gameplay benefits.

Our main survival world never resets. We expand the world border as needed. Special event worlds may be temporary, but we always give at least 2 weeks notice before any world resets. Player inventories and currency are preserved across any resets.

We regularly recruit staff from our active community members. Requirements include:

- 3+ months as an active community member
- Clean disciplinary record
- Demonstrated maturity and helpfulness

When applications open, we announce them in Discord. Most staff start as Helpers before advancing.
